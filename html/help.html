Cryptojacking Detection Help

What is CryptoJacking?

CryptoJacking is a browser attack that functions in the background when you visit infected websites from a browser.

How do I use this extension?

The extension runs in the background monitoring the websites you browse and will notify you if you visit a site that is known to perform CryptoJacking. The attack will be prevented.

Who made this extension?

This extension has been developed as the artefact of a final year undegraduate dissertation. The full source code of the extension is available at google.com
